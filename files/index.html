Software · SMB Service

MissedCallAI

Automated missed-call text-back for trade businesses — plumbers, HVAC, electricians, roofers. When a call goes unanswered, the customer gets a friendly text within five seconds, a GPT-4o agent keeps the conversation focused on intent, address, and availability, and every lead logs to a live document the owner checks between jobs. Built so one recovered lead a month pays for the service many times over. $150/mo, no contracts, 30-day money-back.

Present Breath

A slow video-essay channel on atmospheric games and the technology behind their grip — meditative horror, immersive sims, GPU architecture, sound as space. Anti-clickbait, anti-tier-list, anti-hype. Thai Learner

IA Redesign — Less choices, more learning.

A proposed restructure of Thai Learner from a feature-rich dashboard to a focused four-tab architecture. Each tab answers a different question the user is asking themselves: what should I do now, where am I in my journey, let me explore, and stay out of the way. Built around how returning users actually behave, with a path to monetization wired in.

About

Solo builder, Bangkok-based.

I'm Aaron. I build things — mostly software, sometimes images, occasionally writing — and I do it solo, mostly to scratch my own itches first. The current itches: a Thai-learning app that doesn't suck, an AI agent that handles the calls a small business can't, a visual practice that looks like nothing else on a feed.

I'm interested in language acquisition, the geometry of game aesthetics, the way small craft compounds into something coherent over years, and how to ship things to the audiences they'd actually serve — not the audiences a launch deck imagines.

I work in public when I can. This page is the index. Each project links out to its own overview when one exists.
