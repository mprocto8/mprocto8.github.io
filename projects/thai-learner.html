The goal

Conversational Thai, not memorization.

Most language apps teach you to recognize words. Few teach you to produce them. Thai Learner is built around the premise that real fluency comes from internalizing patterns, hearing native pronunciation, and practicing active recall — not collecting flashcards.

01

Speak, don't just read

Active production over passive recognition. Typing Challenge and Pattern Practice force you to construct Thai from scratch, the way you'd need to in conversation.

02

Learn the structure, not surface

A pattern-based system teaches grammatical frames you can fill with any vocabulary — generating infinite real sentences from a small set of mastered patterns.

03

Hear it the way Thais say it

Pre-recorded native-quality audio for every word and sentence. Tones, vowel lengths, and natural rhythm — the things tonal languages depend on but text alone can't convey.

04

Build through use, not study

Streaks, daily review queues, and a mistake deck that surfaces exactly what you need to reinforce. The app remembers what you've struggled with so you don't have to.

The method

Three layers of real fluency.

Linguistic research and personal experience converge on the same insight: conversational ability requires three distinct things working together. The app is structured around them.

i.

Patterns — the grammatical scaffolding

~25 core patterns like [X] อยู่ที่ไหน ("Where is X?") that you can fill with any noun. Master the frames, generate infinite sentences. This is the productive engine of Thai conversation.

ii.

Vocabulary — the high-frequency fillers

The 200 most-used words that plug into the patterns. Daily-life nouns, common verbs, essential adjectives. Curated for usefulness, not academic completeness.

iii.

Chunks — the idiomatic phrases

Fixed expressions like ไม่เป็นไร ("don't worry about it") that don't follow patterns and just need to be memorized whole. Cultural knowledge encoded as language.

What's built

Eight ways to learn, one app.

Each mode targets a different learning channel — visual, audio, kinesthetic, productive. Together they form a feedback loop that mirrors how real conversation builds fluency.

Recognition

Match Game

Tap pairs of English and Thai to match. Audio plays on success. Quick wins for early confidence.

Spaced Repetition

Flashcards

3D flip cards with bucket-based scheduling. Audio buttons on both faces and example sentences.

Speed

Speed Round

60-second timed drill. Personal bests per topic. Pressure-tests recall under time constraint.

Audio-First

Listen & Choose

Hear Thai, identify English. Variable speed playback. The primary mode for tone training.

Production

Typing Challenge

Type Thai romanization from English prompts. Lenient matching forgives tone marks and spacing.

Generative

Pattern Practice

Multi-position slot fill teaches grammatical structures. Three-clip audio choreography reinforces every answer.

Construction

Sentence Builder

Drag-and-arrange words into grammatical sentences. Per-word audio on tap. Full sentence playback on completion.

Specialized

Time & Tone Trainer

Dedicated drills for telling time in Thai and the five tones — the two foundational systems Thai requires.
